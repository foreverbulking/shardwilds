# World Design

## World Model

Shardwilds uses a hybrid world:

- Shared open-world zones
- Instanced solo/co-op dungeons
- PvP frontier zones
- Housing/farm neighborhoods later

## Zone Types

```txt
Town: safe social hub
Open World: shared PvE/gathering zone
Dungeon: instanced PvE zone
PvP Frontier: contested high-reward zone
Housing: designated plot zone
```

## Initial Regions

### Hearthvale

Starter town and surrounding fields.

Purpose:

- New player spawn
- Farming
- Fishing
- Basic crafting
- First trade route
- Safe social play

PvP:

- Disabled

### Slimewood

Beginner forest.

Purpose:

- Wood
- Herbs
- Beginner monsters
- Hidden resource nodes

PvP:

- Disabled or optional event only

### Coppercap Hills

Mining region.

Purpose:

- Copper
- Stone
- Cave monsters
- First dungeon

PvP:

- Disabled early

### Sunscale Coast

Fishing and trade port.

Purpose:

- Fishing
- Salt
- Pearls
- Trade delivery
- Ships later

PvP:

- Disabled early

### Thornfen Marsh

Dangerous PvE region.

Purpose:

- Rare herbs
- Elite monsters
- Poison materials

PvP:

- Optional later

### Red Banner Frontier

PvP resource zone.

Purpose:

- Rare ore
- High-value trade route
- PvP conflict

PvP:

- Enabled

## First Dungeon: Copperroot Burrow

Type:

- Solo/co-op instance

Length:

- 8 to 12 minutes

Enemies:

- Cave jellies
- Mushroom imps
- Copper beetles

Boss:

- Burrowbeetle King

Rewards:

- Copper beetle shell
- Glowing fungus
- Burrow resin
- Beginner recipe
- Rare cosmetic
